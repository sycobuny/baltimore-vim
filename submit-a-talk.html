Use the form below to submit a talk!

We're looking for technical topics on Vim or other text editor/IDE solutions. We may run a "soft" topic such as community building, but this is at the discretion of the organizer(s).

Please note, there is no guarantee all talks will be accepted, but we'll do our best to make sure everyone who wants to will get a chance to speak!

Additionally, please make sure you've read the Code of Conduct, speakers have to adhere to it too!
