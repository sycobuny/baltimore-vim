Suggested Topics

If you've wanted to stand up and speak your mind, and got yourself all the way to our talk submission page, only to find that you couldn't think of just what to talk about, you may be in luck. This page will list various topics that members have indicated they'd like to hear about in the future, and if it turns out you know anything about them, you've got your seed of an idea right there!

As the group is fairly new, we haven't received any suggested topics yet, but feel free to check back here any time to see if you've got something to say and you just don't know it yet!

Also, if you have an idea for a talk that you'd like to hear, send a tweet my way (@sycobuny on Twitter), and I'll add it to this page!
