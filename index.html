Welcome to the home of the Baltimore Vim user group.

We're a group of people enthusiastic about the Vim text editor, and who want to know more about how to get the most from it.

Location and meeting times:

Meetings will take place in the classroom area of the Village Learning Place (in the office building across the street at 2510 St. Paul) in Charles Village, Baltimore:

If the embedded map above does not work, please try this link to Google Maps: https://goo.gl/maps/b45aN, or enter the following information into your GPS of choice:

We will generally meet on the third Wednesday of the month, from 6:30-7:30 PM, with a "Social Hour" at a local walking-distance venue. Depending on venue and organizer availability, this schedule may need to be periodically tweaked, so make sure you're signed up with our Meetup page, so you can receive notifications (if you want) about our schedule. It is also the first page to receive updates when we schedule our Meetups, so it's the happening place for piping-hot news!

About the organizer:

Meetings are organized by Stephen Belcher (@sycobuny on GitHub). Born in Massachussetts but raised in Baltimore, Stephen has presented at local Perl Mongers meetups as well as being a regular presenter at the annual DC/Baltimore Perl Workshop.

Currently he works as a contractor at the National Institute on Aging in the Biomedical Research Center on the Johns Hopkins Bayview campus in East Baltimore, where he writes programs to support scientific research.

In his spare time he plays his harp, occasionally performing with local orchestra and chamber groups, and spends an unhealthy amount of time rewatching old episodes of Mystery Science Theater 3000 with his husband.
