Welcome to the home of the Baltimore Vim user group!

We're a group of people enthusiastic about the Vim text editor, and who want to know more about how to get the most from it.

Next Meetup:

  • RSVP November 18 6:30pm - Technical Hour
  • RSVP November 18 7:45pm - Social Hour

November 18th is our next Meetup!

  • Robert Weisser - Traditional Vi (Intermediate, 20 Minutes)

    Robert will take us through some of the origins of features in Vim that can be traced back to original Vi. While Vim adds some neat bells and whistles on top, many of the most impressive features were simply carried over from the original software that inspired it, and we're gonna learn how to use some.
  • Stephen Belcher - Macros (Advanced, 15 Minutes)

    Even once you've learned how to jump around a file and do large editing operations, you can still find yourself repeating a sequence of commands over and over again. With macros, you can record and replay frequently-used command sequences to power up your editing.

There will also be the usual "Social Hour" meetup following the technical talk and open forum, at Yum's Asian Bistro around the corner from the Meetup space.

General Schedule

Meetings take place in the classroom area of the Village Learning Place (in the office building across the street at 2510 St. Paul) in Charles Village, Baltimore:

We will generally meet on the third our fourth Monday of the month, from 6:30-7:30 PM. There will occasionally be a small amount of local beer and/or pizza from the nearby Charles Restaurant.

Depending on how social everyone feels, some or all of the group can head to the nearby Yum's Asian Bistro after the meetup to continue talking Vim, or just partake of some delicious food and/or drinks. Yum's Asian Bistro is around the corner from the VLP, within easy walking distance.

Depending on venue and organizer availability, this schedule may need to be periodically tweaked, so make sure you're signed up with our Meetup page, so you can receive notifications (if you want) about individual meetups, or simply check back on this page periodically.
