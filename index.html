Location Update!

The Village Learning Place has unfortunately suffered some water damage and we have had to relocate while they repair the damage. The Digital Harbor Foundation has graciously agreed to host us in the meantime. See below for details.

Welcome to the home of the Baltimore Vim user group!

We're a group of people enthusiastic about the Vim text editor, and who want to know more about how to get the most from it. We have (mostly) monthly Meetups with technical presentations and general chat about Vim.

General Schedule

Meetings will generally be taking place in the nanolab of the Digital Harbor Foundation in Federal Hill, Baltimore:

If the embedded map above does not work, please try this link to Google Maps: https://goo.gl/maps/cBNjmvHv6co, or enter the following information into your GPS of choice:

Digital Harbor Foundation
1045 Light St.
Baltimore, MD 21230

We will generally meet on the first or second Monday of the month, from 6:30-7:30 PM. While we're at the Digital Harbor Foundation, there will be no beer as it is an alcohol-free facility, but there should be some tasty pizza and soda!

Depending on how social everyone feels, some or all of the group can head to a nearby Federal Hill bar or restaurant, to continue talking Vim or just partake of some delicious food and/or drinks. Many venues are within immediate walking distance of the DHF.

Due to venue and organizer availability, this schedule may need to be periodically tweaked, so make sure you're signed up with our Meetup page, so you can receive notifications (if you want) about individual meetups, or simply check back on this page periodically.
